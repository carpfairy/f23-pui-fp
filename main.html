blend:the fabric decoder.

blend is here to tell you all about the blend of your fabric composition.

type your fabric content and % as you see it on your clothing tag, or copy paste from the shopping website under "composition".


Instructions:
Find the tag inside of your clothing item. Or, copy paste the fabric content under the "Materials" or "Composition" section of an online shopping site. Type the composition label as you see it ("x% fabric" or "fabric x%") into the text box, separated by a comma.
