Cashmere is one of the best options for cold weather clothing, such as sweaters. It is known for its softness, lightweight warmth, and breathability. It is roughly eight times warmer than sheep's wool. However, it can be expensive and difficult to maintain. Lower quality can be itchy to direct skin.
